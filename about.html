About Beehaus

Beehaus is there for beginners who aren't even sure if they want to adopt some bees for their garden and don't know where to start or what to do, and experts who know exactly what bee they want and want to go straight to the adoption process. There are already over 150 beehaus communities across BC, Canada, together we can help support the Canadian bee population.

Contact us if you have any unanswered bee questions not addressed in our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) page or other pages.

Our Pledge

Our History

Established in 2014 after reports of the decline of the bee population, we decided it was time to take action and help by providing information on bee care and an easy way to adopt bees.
