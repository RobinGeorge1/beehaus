Welcome to Beehaus

Here at Beehaus you can adopt your own bee family in their ready to go nest from one of the three breeds that we home. All our bees are great pollinators and will help your beautiful garden grow. Based on your location we can even recommend which bee is best for you. You can further learn about them below.

Our Adoptable Bees

Mason Bee

Named after the mud they use to line their homes, mason bees are typically sought after by gardeners with fruit trees, but they are also very helpful with pollinating flowers. Solitary and non-aggresive, mason bees are a wonderful addition to any garden.

Learn More

Leafcutter Bee

To line their nests, leafcutter bees will take small, circular sections of leaves and overlap them within a small found cavity. No need to worry about the cuttings damaging the leafs or flowers, as their strong pollinating power more than makes up for it.

Learn More

Mining Bee

Do have sandy soil in your backyard? Then these bees would love to be adopted by you. As the name implies, mining bees reside underground, leaving a little mound on the surface. They are a lovely looking breed of bees, with the females being reddish orange.
