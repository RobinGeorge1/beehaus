Welcome to Beehaus

What You Can Do Here

Here at Beehaus you can adopt your own bee family in their ready to go nest from one of the three breeds that we home. All our bees are great pollinators and will help your beautiful garden grow. Based on your location we can even recommend which bee is best for you in our adoption process. You can further learn about them below.
