Shipping

We ship our bees in packages of frozen beehives. Each hive is contained in an environmentaly-friendly tube that can be installed in your garden straight away. Bees are shipped via priority mail and may take up to 5 business days to deliver. A flat rate of $9.99 is charged for each order.
